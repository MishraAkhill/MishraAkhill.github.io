FOUNDER- ADIRAJ CHARITABLE SOCIETY


  • Adiraj charitable society is an NGO formed to help the unprivileged group of the society. The primary goal of the society is to focus on providing the basic amenities and education to the children below the age of 15. Children below such age are the upcoming bloom of our country which needs correct guidance and basic education along with proper food in order to make our country free from poverty, malnutrition and access to quality education. The above mentioned purpose can be fulfilled by spreading awareness among the parents and by providing them enough support through which their children are not forced to work or deprived of education due to work. We have ascertained certain new measures other than that of opted by governments. We, to this age group are aiming to provide not only the books but want them to explore their inner consensus, want them to understand the real world and not just the world of books. Children of 5-12 years of age group are very much keen to learn and show their talents which remains out of their own sight due to other problems running out in their lives.
